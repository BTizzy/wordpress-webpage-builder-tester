🎯 Trillium Website Preview Setup

✅ Step 1: Preview Your New Design Locally

To see your new website design before putting it on WordPress:

  1. Rename trillium_styles.css to styles.css
  2. Rename trillium_script.js to script.js
  3. Open trillium_homepage.html in your browser

This will show you exactly what your website will look like!

📋 What You'll See:

  • Clean black, white, grey design with gold accents
  • Smooth animations and hover effects
  • Professional hero section with your value proposition
  • Service cards for Small Business & Startup consulting
  • Client testimonials grid
  • Contact forms and newsletter signup
  • Mobile-responsive navigation menu
